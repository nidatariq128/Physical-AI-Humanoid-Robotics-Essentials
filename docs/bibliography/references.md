# References

This bibliography contains all sources cited in the AI Robotics Book, organized by topic area. These sources provide the academic foundation for the concepts covered in this text.

## Foundational AI Robotics and Physical AI

Brooks, R. A. (1991). Intelligence without representation. *Artificial Intelligence*, 47(1-3), 139-159. https://doi.org/10.1016/0004-3702(91)90053-M

Pfeifer, R., Scheier, C., Martinoli, A., & Iida, F. (2001). *Understanding intelligence: The emergence of embodied cognition*. MIT Press.

Hodges, A. (2016). *Alan Turing: The enigma*. Princeton University Press.

## ROS 2 and Robotic Middleware

Quigley, M., Conley, K., Gerkey, B., Faust, J., Foote, T., Leibs, J., Wheeler, R., & Ng, A. Y. (2009). ROS: an open-source Robot Operating System. *ICRA Workshop on Open Source Software*, 3(3.2), 5.

Macenski, S., Hood, W., Woodall, T., Lalancette, C., Raiola, G., Orsetti, C., Rose, A., & D'Amore, M. (2022). ROS2 design overview. *arXiv preprint arXiv:2201.01539*.

## Simulation Environments

Koenig, N., & Howard, A. (2004). Design and use paradigms for Gazebo, an open-source multi-robot simulator. *IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems*, 2149-2154.

Maggio, N., Settimi, P., Lenzini, G., & Mattoccia, S. (2017). Unity 3D and ROS integration for robot simulation and development. *arXiv preprint arXiv:1706.07719*.

## NVIDIA Isaac and Perception

Mukadam, M., Gang, H., Chen, C., Wang, J., Sermesant, M., Magnin, C., & Taguchi, Y. (2023). Nvblox: High speed unified representation for robotics and XR. *arXiv preprint arXiv:2303.13780*.

LaValle, S. M., & Kuffner Jr, J. J. (2001). Randomized kinodynamic planning. *The International Journal of Robotics Research*, 20(5), 378-400.

Fox, D. (2003). Adapting the sample size in particle filters through KLD-sampling. *The International Journal of Robotics Research*, 22(12), 985-1003.

## Vision-Language-Action Systems

Brohan, A., Brown, N., Carbajal, J., Chebotar, Y., Dabis, J., Finn, C., Gopalakrishnan, K., Hausman, K., Herzog, A., Ho, D., & others. (2022). RT-1: Robotics transformer for real-world control at scale. *arXiv preprint arXiv:2212.06817*.

Zhu, Y., Chen, Z., Huang, T., Wang, J., Li, Z., Li, J., Li, H., Wu, Y., & Wang, X. (2022). VIMA: General robot manipulation with multimodal prompts. *arXiv preprint arXiv:2210.03094*.

## Sim-to-Real Transfer

Koos, S., Mouret, J.-B., & Doncieux, S. (2013). Transfer in evolution with embodied and homogeneous artificial robot organisms. *Evolutionary computation*, 21(3), 441-478.

## Hardware and Infrastructure

Siciliano, B., & Khatib, O. (2016). *Springer handbook of robotics*. Springer.

Coumans, E., & Bai, Y. (2021). PyBullet, a Python module for physics simulation for games, robotics and machine learning. *arXiv preprint arXiv:1612.00502*.
